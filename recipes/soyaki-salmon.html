Soyaki Salmon

Description

So easy. So delicious. Add over rice or noodles and some steamed veggies for a low-effort dinner at the end of a high-effort day.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Soyaki sauce from Trader Joe's

Steps

  1. Line your pan with foil. Pour soyaki sauce over the salmon.
  2. For an air fryer, use "air roast" at 375F for 15 minutes or until fully cooked. Time depends on how big your salmon pieces are.
  3. Profit. (The sugar from the sauce may blacken and burn on the pan but it's totally fine. Use foil for easy clean up)
