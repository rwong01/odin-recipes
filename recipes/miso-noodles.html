Miso and Garlic Butter Noodles

Description

Addictive, savory noodles so easy you'll be regretting that you didn't make these sooner. Garlic, miso, and butter were a match made in heaven. Make a vat for meal prep and add mushrooms and protein throughout the week. I swear these noods don't get old.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 ounces noodles
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 5-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon miso
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • sesame seeds for color (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles as you normally would. Drain, but save a cup of pasta water.
  2. Melt the butter and add in the pasta water, garlic, and miso.
  3. Toss in the noodles and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds
