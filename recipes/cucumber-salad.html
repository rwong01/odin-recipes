Cucumber Salad

Description

A tasty, tangy, slightly spicy cucumber salad dupe of the one from the famouse Din Tai Fung chain. Make ahead of time and for an easy dinner party hit. Chill and marinate in the fridge overnight for the most flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Persian cucumbers
  • 2 tbsp Reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 cloves Garlic, smashed or minced
  • 1/2 tbsp Chili oil
  • 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp Salt

Steps

  1. Chop cucumbers into ½” rounds and place in a large bowl. Sprinkle with salt to draw out moisture and let sit for at least 10 minutes. Drain excess water and pat dry completely.
  2. Mix everything else together to make your sauce.
  3. Add in the cucumbers. Let marinate for a few minutes or overnight. Enjoy!
